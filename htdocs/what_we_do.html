Who We Are / What We Do

We provide consultative and software development services for the integration and implementation of application systems.

Our development services deliver solutions in systems integration, a primary practice, and in closing functional and infrastructural gaps as well. We architect complete enterprise systems with a view toward seamless, transparent interaction and data sharing among packaged and bespoke software components.

Our implementation services range through software selection and feasibility, infrastructural configuration, functional implementation and performance benchmarking.

Our implementation teams are efficient and well-practiced in their respective skillsets. They are highly visible, use the most professional techniques, and are completely accountable to their clients' needs and directives.

Around implementations, we also provide project management services, especially those having short start-to-finish timeframes.

Our clients span a broad range of businesses and enterprises, in arenas such as biotechnology, engineering, media, colleges, associations and personnel management.
